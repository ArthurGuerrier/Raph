A composer, orchestrator-copyist, and arranger, I received classical training at the conservatory and university. I studied composition, harmony, counterpoint, orchestration, musical analysis, music history, accordion, piano, singing, and choral conducting. As a music composer and member of SACEM, I regularly collaborate with various directors on their short and feature-length fiction film projects. My work has received awards at the Barcelona Best Film Festival in 2021 and the Barcelona Super Short Film Festival in 2022. I have also worked on reportages and with students at the European Institute of Cinema and Audiovisual (IECA) in Nancy on their final projects. I also produce stock music in France and abroad (Norway, Czech Republic, Japan, etc.). Several of my tracks have been selected by the Association of Music Supervisors (ASM) at the 2023 Cannes Sync Awards, as well as by the Union of Advertising Sound Producers (SPSP) and the Sync Summit (New York, Hollywood, and Paris).
