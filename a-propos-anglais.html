Composer, orchestrator-copyist and arranger, I followed classical courses at the conservatory and at university. I studied composition, harmony, counterpoint, orchestration, musical analysis, music history, accordion, piano, singing and choir direction.

As a music composer and member of the SACEM (the french Society of Authors, Composers and Music Publishers), I regularly collaborate with different directors on their short and feature film projects. My work was rewarded at the Barcelona Best Film Festival in 2021 and at the Barcelona Super Short Film Festival in 2022. I also worked on reportages and with the students of the European Cinema and Audiovisual Institute (IECA - Institut Européen du Cinéma et de l’Audiovisuel) in Nancy (France) on their school final productions.

I’m also a producer of music library in France and abroad (Norway, Czech Republic, Japan, ...). Several of my works were selected by the french Music Supervisors Association (ASM - Association des Superviseurs Musicaux) for the Cannes Sync Award 2023, the french Union of Sound Producers for Advertising (SPSP - Syndicat des Producteurs de Sons pour la Publicité) and the Sync Summit (New York City, Hollywood and Paris).
